council member andrew j. medellin
madera city council district 2 district 2

Andrew “Andy” Medellin was first appointed to serve on the Madera City Council on July 1, 2011 to fill a vacant seat on the City Council for a term through December 5, 2012. In 2012, Andy successfully ran for office in Council District 2 and was elected to serve a four year term through 2016.

In 2010, the method by which Council Members are elected to office changed from members elected at large to members elected by district with an elected mayor. The first election by district with an elected mayor was held in November of 2012.

Council Members also serve as Board Members of the Successor Agency to the Former Madera Redevelopment Agency and Commissioners of the Housing Authority of the City of Madera.

Andy previously served on the City of Madera Planning Commission from 1998 through 2004 and again from 2007 through 2011. He also served as the Commission’s Chairman for three terms

Andy has been the owner/operator of Andy’s Sports and Design since 1993.
